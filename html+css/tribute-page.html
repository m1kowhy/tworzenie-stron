Marie Curie

Key Facts About Marie Curie:

  • Born on November 7, 1867 in Warsaw, Poland
  • First woman to win a Nobel Prize
  • Only person to win Nobel Prizes in two different scientific fields (Physics and Chemistry)
  • Pioneered research on radioactivity
  • Discovered the elements polonium and radium
  • Established the Curie Institutes in Paris and Warsaw
  • Died on July 4, 1934 from aplastic anemia caused by radiation exposure
